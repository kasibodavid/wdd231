Site Plan - ExploreNaija Adventures

Discover Nigeria’s hidden gems and breathtaking adventures.

Site Name

ExploreNaija Adventures – This name reflects our mission to help people explore the beauty, culture, and attractions of Nigeria. “Naija” is a popular local nickname for Nigeria, making the name friendly and relatable.

Optional domain availability: explorenaija.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a travel and tourism hub, providing destination guides, tour booking options, cultural event information, and travel tips for exploring Nigeria’s cities, nature reserves, and beaches.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist destinations to visit in Lagos, Abuja, and Calabar?
  • How can I book a guided tour for Obudu Mountain Resort?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #006400 – Used for headings, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons (represents nature and adventure).

Secondary Color: #F4A300 – Used for highlights, links, and accents (represents warmth and energy).

Background Color: #F5F5F5 – Used for main background for better readability.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif – Clean and modern, used for all main titles and headings.

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – Easy to read, used for paragraphs, captions, and general text.
